Personicle - the personal chronicle

Personicle is a person centric healthcare and lifestyle data platform that logs information about lifestyle, health, social, environmental, and other aspects of a person's life to provide highly personalized & preventive health insights in real-time. Our mission is to provide a complete view of a person's life style and health and enable individuals and healthcare providers to make informed decisions in clinical and real-world setting.

Leverage your data

You are generating a large amount of data about your habits and lifestyle. Leverage it to get a complete view of your health and life.

Identify your habits

Users can aggregate their events and find out how much time was spent in different activities. This can empowers users to identify their long-term habits and align their actions with the goals.

Understand yourself

Personicle can leverage the daily events and information about user's daily lives to identify the situations that trigger outcomes of interest. This enables users to get a better understanding of their behavior and health and achieve their life and health goals.
